Kevin Lin

I am a second-year PhD student at UC Berkeley working with Joey Gonzalez and Dan Klein. I'm broadly interested in natural language processing and machine learning. I am affiliated with Berkeley NLP, BAIR, and RISE. My research is supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Previously, I was part of AI2's AllenNLP team and before that I was an undergraduate in the WuLab.
